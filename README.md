### This is a roguelike action RPG where you play as a magical forest courier — race against the clock, fight through enchanted wilderness, and slowly unravel the secrets of the world you call home.

The game's defining feature is time management — you must complete each stage within a time limit. Using various techniques consumes time, and certain enemies' special attacks will also reduce your remaining time.
